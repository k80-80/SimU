‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

  • üè¢ Can I work and study at the same time?
    Yes! SimU is an online school, leaving you free to juggle a full or part time job to make ends meet.
  • üéí Can I study multiple degrees simultaneously?
    You can only study one degree at a time, but the registration fee is waived for returning students looking to join a new degree program.
  • üìà Can my Sim fail a degree?
    There is no failure state. Outcomes reflect effort, not perfection.
  • üöå Can teens get a degree?
    Teens can register for a degree, but only once they've either graduated High School, or earned their GED online. If your Sim is currently in school, and you don't have the High School Years DLC, I've included an option under Computer ‚Üí High School to quit. Then your Sim can enroll and earn their GED.
  • üîé Do I need to register for a degree just to access the Online Skills feature?
    No. Just like the original version of Online Skills, you can Browse Digital Study Guides from the Computer whenever you like. More involved LearnLab Classes are available at Level 2 of the Academic Aptitude Skill, and a Phone option at Level 6.
  • üí° Does the Academic Aptitude Skill add anything?
    The included Skill is custom, and revolves around learning and scholarly practice. It can be increased by taking online classes, and using the Academic Resource Hub, and unlocks multiple exclusive interactions.
  • üö© Does this mod conflict with others that add Career Jumps?
    No, the career jumps are injected into loot on join, meaning your Sim will join the career, and instantly be offered the chance for a higher position due to their degree.
  • üéì Does this mod require Discover University?
    No! SimU is 100% Base Game Compatible. All you need to get started is the mod itself, my modlibrary, and Lot 51's Core Library.
  • üíª How do I know where to complete the assignments?
    Hover your mouse over the assignment in the career panel, and you'll see the instructions for completion.
  • üóíÔ∏è I accidentally canceled the coursework module.
    You'll need to restart the module from the SimU Computer menu.
  • üëæ Is there a cheat to add a degree to Sims?

  • üíº What are the career benefits for each degree?
    From the SimU menu on a Phone or Computer, View Degrees to see a list of every career that the degrees benefit. All of EA's careers are included to date, plus my own custom careers if you have them installed.
