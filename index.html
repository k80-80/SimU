⚠️ Important Information

🗓️ Updated to the July 2026 version of SimU?

If your Sim is already partway through a module, you don't need to start over. The new version requires 6 Coursework Credits (1 credit per completed assignment) to unlock the Write Module Paper or Write Dissertation assignment.

To sync your current progress:

  1. Press Ctrl + Shift + C to open the cheat console.
  2. Enter: stats.set_stat midnitetech:simu_statistic_objectiveprogress X
  3. Replace X with the number of assignments your Sim has already completed.

Example: If your Sim has already finished 4 assignments, enter: stats.set_stat midnitetech:simu_statistic_objectiveprogress 4

Your Sim will then only need to complete 2 more assignments before the final paper or dissertation becomes available.

🎓 How SimU Works
    SimU is a brand new academic experience that can be completed from wherever you are in SimNation; this university is 100% online. Degrees are structured around six academic modules, completed sequentially. Each module represents a focused area of study within your chosen degree program.

    No deadlines; Sims may progress steadily or take breaks as needed. Coursework can be completed at any time, and extra studying and quality work can lead to an Honors degree. SimU rewards dedication, not micromanagement.


    NEW: You can now also take a 3-module course, perfect for shorter lifespans. You'll be prompted upon payment.

✍Registration
Head to a Computer, or Phone → Household, and find the SimU menu. Registration is §500, and covers access to the SimU Academic Resource Hub and degree enrollment options.

💲Tuition & Fees
Tuition fees are paid per degree and may be settled upfront, per term, or through approved student loan options. All financial interactions are managed through the SimU menu on a computer or phone app (under Household). Once paid, Sims can choose their degree and will receive their SimU Coursework Notebook in their inventory. This is essential for completing assignments and optional Extra Credit studies.

📘 Coursework & Modules

    Each SimU degree consists of three or six modules dependent on your playstyle, each focusing on a specific academic theme.

    Modules include:

    • A set of coursework assignments
    • Optional studying opportunities
    • Skill-building activities
    • Performance tracking toward final outcomes

    Modules may be worked through at any pace. There is no requirement to “keep up"; progress is entirely in the Sim’s control. 

    📓 Coursework Assignments
    Assignments represent applied academic tasks such as research, writing, analysis, or practical fieldwork. These may be completed using:

    • The SimU Coursework Notebook
    • A computer
    • In-world activities relevant to the subject

    Once completed, assignments can be

    • Proofread
    • Edited
    • Submitted for assessment

    In addition to required coursework, Sims may engage in optional studying, which can increase their chances of graduating with Honors.

    They can email their Professor to ask for Extra Credit assignments from the SimU menu. Optional studying is never mandatory, but it is rewarded.

🧠 Academic Progress & Outcomes

    📈Module Performance
    Each module contributes to the Sim’s overall academic record. After the first module is complete, performance can be checked from the SimU menu or by emailing the professor and is influenced by the following:

    • Assignment quality
    • Optional study effort
    • Consistency across modules

    ✅️Final Exam
    At the conclusion of the final module, Sims may take their Final Exam. Preparation is strongly encouraged; Sims who study tend to fare better. Study for the test in the Student Notebook or on the Computer. Sims will have to wait a while for the results to come in! Try not to panic in the meantime.

    🎓Degree Classification
    Upon graduation, Sims are awarded either

    • Standard Degree
    • Honors Degree

    Honors outcomes reflect sustained effort across the entire program, not a single moment of brilliance.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions
    • 👾 Are there any cheats for SimU?
      You can register for free by Shift+Clicking your Sim’s computer and selecting SimU Cheats. From there, you can use Complete Current Module to skip ahead to the next module. You can also Shift+Click your Sim to add a degree trait if you’d prefer to bypass the entire process.

    • 🏢 Can I work and study at the same time?
      Yes! SimU is an online school, leaving you free to juggle a full- or part-time job to make ends meet.

    • 🎒Can I study multiple degrees simultaneously?
      You can only study one degree at a time, but the registration fee is waived for returning students looking to join a new degree program.

    • 📈Can my Sim fail a degree?
      There is no failure state. Outcomes reflect effort, not perfection. 

    • 🚌Can teens get a degree?
      Teens can register for a degree, but only once they've either graduated High School, or earned their GED online. If your Sim is currently in school, and you don't have the High School Years DLC, I've included an option under Computer → High School to quit. Then your Sim can enroll and earn their GED.

    • 🔎Do I need to register for a degree just to access the Online Skills feature?
      No. Just like the original version of Online Skills, you can Browse Digital Study Guides from the Computer whenever you like. More involved LearnLab Classes are available at Level 2 of the Academic Aptitude Skill and a Phone option at Level 6.

    • 🚩Does this mod conflict with others that add Career Jumps?
      No, the career jumps are injected into loot on join, meaning your Sim will join the career and instantly be offered the chance for a higher position due to their degree.

    • 🎓Does this mod require Discover University? Does it override Discover University?
      No! SimU is 100% Base Game Compatible. All you need to get started is the mod itself, and my modlibrary. Academic Life doesn't touch Discover University's tuning, so you can still play as usual as well if you have it installed.
      A hidden loot is included, however, so that Sims in the DU Academic aspiration will have their progress logged without overriding any tuning.

    • 💻How do I know where to complete the assignments?
      Hover your mouse over the assignment in the career panel, and you'll see the instructions for completion.

    • 📜How long do I have to wait for my Degree/GED to arrive?
      It will arrive by mail within a couple of days.

    • 🕒How long do I have to wait for my Dissertation and/or Final Exam results?
      I've reduced the time down to just a few in-game hours. The results will arrive automatically.

    • ⚙️Is this mod compatible with Kuttoe's Career Overhaul mod?
      Yes, I've included an override for the degree objectives that includes mine and the degrees from Discover University.

    • 💻The next assignment isn't showing up. Where is it?
      If an assignment depends on a previous task (e.g. Submit Essay requires Write Essay) and you’ve used UI Cheats or similar to complete it, you’ll need to cancel the module using the trash can icon and restart it via the SimU computer menu.

    • 💼What are the career benefits for each degree?
      From the SimU menu on a Phone or Computer, View Degrees to see a list of every career that the degrees benefit. All of EA's careers are included to date, plus my own custom careers if you have them installed.

    • 📓What happens if I lose the notebook?
      A replacement can be ordered from the SimU Online Store at any time.

    • 💬 What if my question isn't answered here?
      No worries, the quickest way to get help is on my Discord, or you can check out the support options on my website.

    • 💡What is the Academic Aptitude Skill?
      The included Skill is custom and revolves around learning and scholarly practice. It can be increased by taking online classes and using the Academic Resource Hub and unlocks multiple exclusive interactions.
